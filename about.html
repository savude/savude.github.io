About Me

My Internship

I interned at BUMA Holdings Limited, which is an independent sales agency that specializes in marketing and distributing consumables in the printing and packaging industry. The firm focuses on providing clear solutions for today’s demanding flexo, gravure and screen applications. To this end, the firm is a major supplier of doctor blades in the East and Central African markets. Doctor Blades can be used to print and coat paper, films such as PET, BOPP, PVC, and foils used in packaging, labelling, publications, corrugated products and displays, metal decoration and printed electronics.
