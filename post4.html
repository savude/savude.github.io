Blogging is hard

Posted by Ellen Savude on July 29, 2018

I mostly wanted a technical blog, with an occasional sprinkle of life in it, like a little about my experiences, my own personal insights on different topics not specifically in the Computing realm or something…Different, you know? But mostly technical. Because I am going to be graded on this after all. (Bonus points for my honestly please). Only problem is, I really don’t know how to phrase technical things and after going through tons of content online on tech blogs, I feel out of my depth and overwhelmed by the prospect of creating content of a subject I’m not fully proficient in. The technical blogs in comparison are comprehensive high level essays really, providing commentaries on different features, summarized examples and are super technical (I tend to not understand a lot of what they say and find myself in a loop of searching for what that word means then not understanding something in the definition to watching cat videos on YouTube – the transition is extreme). Anyway, I wanted MY blog to be a reflection of me, which for the most part is silly – in a funny way – but also informative.

So please bear with me as I take on this task and hopefully enlighten you (pretentious much?) on the journey of creating this website. Or maybe not because speaking of all the steps you took to do something is arduous work that at this moment in time, I also can’t seem to come up with the correct vocab to describe. Bear with me.
